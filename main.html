GOA


Goa is a vibrant coastal destination in India known for its stunning beaches, lively nightlife, rich culture, and Portuguese heritage. It offers a perfect mix of relaxation and adventure.

  1. Baga Beach Known for its lively atmosphere and water sports.
  2. Fort Aguada A historic fort with an amazing view of the Arabian Sea.
  3. Chapora Fort Famous for its sunset views and Bollywood fame.
  4. Dudhsagar Falls A majestic waterfall surrounded by lush greenery.
  5. Basilica of Bom Jesus A UNESCO site showcasing baroque architecture.
  • Goan Fish Curry – A spicy and tangy seafood delight.
  • Vindaloo – A flavorful pork dish with Goan spices.
  • Bebinca – A traditional layered dessert.
  • Prawn Balchão – A spicy prawn pickle popular among seafood lovers.
  • Sorpotel – A spicy pork curry served during festivals.
